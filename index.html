How You Earn

Neolife Compensation Plan

Neolife's fair and rewarding plan gives you 5 ways to earn. The more you grow your network and accumulate PV, the higher your bonuses. This is how members build real financial freedom.

1. Retail & Member Profit

Earn immediately by selling Neolife products. Keep the difference between the Suggested Retail Price (SRP) and your Distributor Price (DP).

2. Sales Volume Bonus (SVB)

Earn up to 25% on your purchases and your downline's purchases. Requires 100 Personal PV and 250 Qualified PV minimum to qualify each month.

3. Leadership Development Bonus

Once you become a Director (4,000 QPV), earn bonuses up to 6% on three levels of qualified Directors you develop in your team.

4. Network Development Bonus

At President's Team rank, earn up to 3% bonus on your entire unlimited network. Combined with LDB, this means up to 9% on qualified Directors.
